Leap City – Parkour Game

Third-person 3D parkour game featuring multi-level chases and movement-based challenges.

Overview

Leap City is a 3D parkour challenge where players must escape through a series of progressively harder levels. Each stage tests movement, timing, and environmental awareness as the player outruns threats and navigates unique obstacles.

The game includes four fully playable levels, each with its own theme, hazards, and visual style.

Levels

  • Level 1 – City Alleyway: Teaches basic movement and timing.
  • Level 2 – Riverside Run: Environmental hazards and tighter jumps.
  • Level 3 – Rooftop Escape: Building-to-building leaps and higher difficulty.
  • Level 4 – Zoo Pursuit: Dynamic level featuring a tiger chase.

Core Mechanics

Movement System

  • Custom movement script with animation controller integration.
  • Context-based animations for running, jumping, and falling.

Game Loop

  • Timer system determines win/loss conditions.
  • Falling or running out of time triggers Game Over.
  • Completing objectives sends the player to a level complete scene.

Collectibles

  • Coins placed strategically throughout levels.
  • Each coin adds 100 points to the final score.

Development Contributions

  • Level design for Levels 1–3.
  • Player movement script and animation connections.
  • Main title screen, level select, collectibles, and game over screens.

Assets & Art Style

  • Low-poly character, animal, and environment packs from Unity Asset Store.
  • Stylized consistent look across all four levels.
  • Lighting and time-of-day changes for atmosphere and difficulty variation.
