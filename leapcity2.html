LeapCity v2 – Refined Parkour Experience

A more defined, more polished upgrade of the original LeapCity — rebuilt for smoother movement, cleaner levels, and improved progression.

Work in Progress

Overview

LeapCity v2 is the upgraded version of the original parkour game. Instead of being a test sandbox, this version focuses on fully refining the core game: smoother movement, more defined level layouts, better pacing, and a more professional feel.

The goal of this version is to rebuild the project with higher quality standards, clearer level identity, better challenges, and a stronger overall player experience.

What’s Improved

  • More polished and consistent movement feel.
  • Cleaner, more intentional level blockouts.
  • Improved progression and difficulty curves.
  • Better camera, timing, and movement blending.
  • Foundations for collectibles, challenges, and replay value.

Current Status

LeapCity v2 is still early in development, but movement and level foundations are already more solid than the first version.

  • Movement is partially updated but noticeably smoother.
  • Early versions of Level 1 and Level 2 exist.
  • UI and unlock system are planned but not implemented yet.
  • Collectibles and speedrun modes will be added later.

Planned Additions

Movement

  • Refined sliding, dashing, and double-jump mechanics.
  • Wall-running / wall interactions (optional).
  • Better air control and movement animations.

Level Design

  • 5 levels minimum, each with a unique mechanic.
  • Optimized difficulty progression.

Download Status

LeapCity v2 is not ready for a public build yet. Once the core movement and Level 1 are polished, a downloadable version will be posted.
